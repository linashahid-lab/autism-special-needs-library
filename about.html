About Lina

I am a Victorian registered teacher with an Advanced Master's degree in Special Education and a passion for inclusive education.

This website has been created to share adapted versions of my university essays and research in a way that is practical, accessible and useful for teachers, education support staff, allied health professionals, parents, carers and university students.

My aim is to bridge the gap between academic research and everyday classroom practice by turning university assignments into easy-to-read articles that highlight evidence-based strategies and practical ideas.

Topics include autism, dyslexia, inclusive education, behaviour support, sensory needs, communication, literacy and supporting students with additional needs.

Where available, each article also includes a link to my original university PDF so readers can explore the complete assignment and reference list.

I hope this website becomes a free resource that supports educators and families while encouraging inclusive practice for all learners.
